Common questions

What is BWR plywood?

BWR (boiling-water-resistant) plywood handles humidity and occasional moisture — a step below BWP. It's ideal for general furniture and semi-wet interiors.

Is Canva Ply good for wardrobes?

Yes — Canva BWR on a gurjan face is a strong, cost-effective choice for wardrobes and everyday interior furniture.
