MR vs BWR vs BWP — which do you need?

MR — Moisture Resistant. Dry interiors: wardrobes, panelling, furniture. Not for wet areas.
BWR — Boiling Water Resistant. Handles humidity & occasional moisture — general furniture, semi-wet zones.
BWP — Boiling Waterproof. Fully waterproof: kitchens, bathrooms, wet areas.
Fire Retardant BWP. Waterproof + fire-retardant core: kitchens, offices, commercial safety zones.
