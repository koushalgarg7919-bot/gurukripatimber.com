What is the difference between texture paint and normal paint?

Texture paint creates a raised, tactile surface pattern for a designer look, while normal paint gives a flat finish. Texture is popular for accent walls and exteriors.

Can texture paint be used outdoors?

Yes — exterior-grade texture paint is weather-resistant and widely used on facades and boundary walls.
