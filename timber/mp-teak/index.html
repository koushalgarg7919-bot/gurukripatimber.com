What is MP teak price per cubic feet today?

MP/CP teak (premium sagwan) runs roughly ₹3,000–₹5,500 per cubic foot depending on girth and grade. WhatsApp +91 76964 49897 for today's exact rate — prices move with the market.

What is the difference between MP teak and imported teak?

MP/CP teak is Indian-grown sagwan with tighter grain and higher oil content — the premium choice. Imported teak (Mexican, Brazilian, African) offers strong performance at a lower price. See our imported teak page for those rates.

Which teak is best for main doors?

For main doors, MP/CP teak is unmatched for grain, oil content and long-term stability.
