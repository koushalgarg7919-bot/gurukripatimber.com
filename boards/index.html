MDF vs HDHMR vs WPC — quick guide

MDF. Smooth & paint-ready. Dry interiors, panelling, painted furniture.
HDHMR / HDWR. Denser, moisture-resistant. Kitchens, wardrobes, screw-holding.
WPC. 100% waterproof & termite-proof. Kitchens, bathrooms, doors & frames.
