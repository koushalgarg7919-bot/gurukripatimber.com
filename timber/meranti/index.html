Common questions

What is marandi (meranti) wood price per cubic feet?

Red meranti (marandi) ranges ₹1,100–₹1,800 per cubic foot by grade and section. WhatsApp +91 76964 49897 for today's rate.

Are meranti and marandi the same wood?

Yes — marandi is the local Punjabi/Hindi name for meranti, the Malaysian hardwood. Same timber, different name.

Red meranti vs yellow meranti — what's the difference?

Red meranti is denser and preferred for frames; yellow meranti is lighter and more economical for internal framing work.
