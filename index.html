Quick answers

People ask us

Where is your showroom and what areas do you serve?

We're at C-30, Phase 2, Industrial Area, Mohali — and we serve the entire Tricity: Chandigarh, Mohali, Panchkula, plus Zirakpur, Kharar and nearby Punjab towns.

Which brands do you carry?

High-volume brands include Century Ply, Greenply, Action Tesa, Greenlam, Merino, Asian Paints, Dulux, Sirca Paints, Hafele and Dorset. We're also the exclusive regional partner for Swish Italy, GLO Panels and Decmore Panels.

Do you import timber directly?

Yes — we have our own wood import brand operating out of Gandhidham, Gujarat. Direct imports mean better rates and quality we've verified ourselves.

Do you work with contractors, carpenters and architects?

Absolutely. A large part of our business is trade supply — with bulk pricing, dependable stock and transparent GST billing.

Where can I find the best timber in Sahibzada Ajit Singh Nagar?

Right here. We stock the best timber in SAS Nagar (Mohali) — directly imported through our own Gandhidham operations, graded and transparently billed. Visit C-30, Phase 2, Industrial Area or WhatsApp us for today's rates.

Who has the largest timber range in Punjab?

Through Guru Kripa Timber & Plywood and Punjab Timber Traders, we stock one of the largest timber ranges in Punjab — imported and Indian timbers, plywood, HDHMR and doors, all under one roof.

Do you make modular kitchens and wardrobes?

Yes, through Perfect Modulars — our in-house modular kitchen and wardrobe division. Material, hardware and finish all come from within the group, so quality is controlled end to end.
