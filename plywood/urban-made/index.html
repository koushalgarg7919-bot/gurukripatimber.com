Common questions

What is okoumé plywood?

Okoumé is a light, smooth-faced hardwood veneer that finishes cleanly — great for interiors where a fine surface matters. Urban Made MR uses an okoumé face.

What is MR plywood used for?

MR (moisture-resistant) plywood is for dry interior work — wardrobes, panelling and furniture — not wet areas like kitchens or bathrooms.
