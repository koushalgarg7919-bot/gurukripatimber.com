What is the difference between WPC and charcoal fluted panels?

WPC fluted panels are denser and moisture-tolerant; charcoal (bamboo-charcoal) panels are lighter and eco-friendly. Both give the same ribbed designer look.

Where are fluted panels used?

TV feature walls, headboards, reception and office walls, and ceiling accents — anywhere you want warm, textured depth.
