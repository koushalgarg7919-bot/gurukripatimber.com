Common questions

What is partal wood?

Partal is the local name for Himalayan spruce/fir softwood — light, straight-grained and economical, used mainly for shuttering, packaging and framing.

What is partal wood price in Punjab?

Partal typically runs ₹550–₹900 per cubic foot depending on size and moisture. WhatsApp us for today's rate and ready sizes.

What is partal wood called in English?

Partal is spruce/fir in English — sometimes loosely called whitewood.
